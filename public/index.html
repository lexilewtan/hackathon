Bringing Boston's Archives To Life

Over the past 150 years, the Boston Public Library has invested tax payer dollars into taking care of the world's greatest

informational treasures.

But even though these collections are being digitally archived, they are virtually hidden from Boston's community of learners.

So we’re putting learning and discovery in the hands of the brightest of the Boston community

- its students.

If anyone can combine the invaluable intelligence of the library with the efficient poetry of clever code, it's young and fearless hackers.

Teams

We’re hand selecting teams of the most creative and tech-savvy students at the best schools in the Boston area.

They will have access to some amazing library apis and data sets, plus top-notch mentorship from the BPL and the Boston technical community. We will post the team rosters in September.

The Event

Then, on Oct 27th - 28th, we're throwing

Knowledge, Hacked

a live display in the BPL dedicated to bringing those secret archives to life. There will be awesome judges, prizes, and of course, the results of everyone’s hard work!
